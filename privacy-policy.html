Workout Cards Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 13, 2026

Workout Cards is a simple workout planning app for creating day-based workout cards and recording sets, reps, and weight.

Data Collection

Workout Cards does not collect, store, sell, share, or transmit personal data to the developer or to any third-party service.

Workout data you enter in the app is stored locally on your device using Apple's local storage technologies. The app does not require an account, does not include analytics, does not use advertising, and does not track you across apps or websites.

Health and Fitness Disclaimer

Workout Cards is provided for general fitness planning and informational purposes only. It does not provide medical, health, physiotherapy, or personal training advice.

Always consult a qualified health professional before starting or changing an exercise program, especially if you have an injury, medical condition, pain, dizziness, or other health concerns.

Changes

This privacy policy may be updated if the app's features or data practices change.

Contact

For support or privacy questions, contact the developer through the support page for Workout Cards.
